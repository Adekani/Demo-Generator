Our Story

From Lagos traffic to global trade lanes

Gakata Global Services was founded in Abuja by logistics operators tired of the opacity in Nigerian freight. We saw SMEs struggling with clearing agents, hidden charges, and shipments that disappeared for weeks.

We built a different model: licensed in-house customs brokerage, direct airline contracts, and a technology platform that gives you real visibility. Today we move everything from fashion imports to oilfield equipment, with offices in Abuja, Lagos, and Port Harcourt.

Our name 'Gakata' means precision in Hausa — it's how we work.
